Bio

I am a first year PhD Candidate at INSA Lyon and I am working in the LIRIS lab (Imagine team). My research focus is on deep learning and computer vision. I am particulary interested in applying deep learning for video understanding. My PhD topic is "Deep learning for human understanding: gestures, activities, poses". I have a funding from the ANR/NSERC project "Deep Vision" which involves 4 parteners: INSA Lyon, LIP6, University of Guelph (Canada) and Simon Fraser University (Canada). I am supervised by Christian Wolf and Julien Mille.

I received an Engineer's degree from ENSAI with a major in Big Data - Data Science. I also have a BSc in Economics from Paris Dauphine University and a BSc in Applied Mathematics from Grenoble Alpes University. I did a 6 months internship at Xerox Research Centre Europe in Grenoble where I worked on Unsupervised Domain Adaptation for object recognition under the supervision of Boris Chidlovskii and Gabriela Csurka.
