I am a first year PhD Candidate at INSA Lyon and I am working in the LIRIS lab (Imagine team). My research focuses on machine learning and computer vision. I have a funding from the ANR/NSERC project "Deep Vision". I am supervised by Christian Wolf and Julien Mille.

I received (soon) an Engineer's degree from ENSAI with a major in Big Data - Data Science. I also have a Bachelor's degree in Economics from Paris Dauphine University and a Bachelor's degree in Applied Mathematics from Grenoble Alpes University.
