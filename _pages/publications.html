Pose-conditioned Spatio-Temporal Attention for Human Action Recognition

We introduce an attention-based mechanism on RGB videos. Given pose features from 3D body joints coordinates our model is able to pay attention at the most discriminative parts of the RGB frames. We report state-of-the-art performance on the largest human action recognition dataset (NTU RGB+D dataset ~ 57'000 videos) using skeleton data and RGB frames. We also successfully perform transfer learning on tiny datasets.
Fabien Baradel, Christian Wolf, Julien Mille
arXiv:1703.10106
